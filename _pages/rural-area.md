<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 24pt;">LACK OF HEALTH SERVICES</span></h2>
<img class="aligncenter" src="http://35.201.11.70/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/landscape-33145-300x150.png" alt="" width="564" height="282" />

<img class=" wp-image-81 aligncenter" src="https://findcaringarea.ga/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/1。1-300x150.png" alt="" width="438" height="219" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Compared to people living in urban areas,</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Australian's living in rural </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">and remote areas </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">tend to </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">have shorter lives,</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">higher changes of illness and injuries,</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">and fewer opportunities to access </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">and use health services.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Poor health outcomes in rural and remote areas</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">may be due to a range of factors,</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">including education </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">and employment</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> opportunities, </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">income, and</span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> access to health </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">services. </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">This imbalance becomes more obvious </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">when  </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">comparing issues such as suicide rates, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">which are</span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">1.7 times higher in remote areas, and </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">the death </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">rate from land transport accidents, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">which in rural </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">areas is 5.4 times the </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">death rate when compared to major cities. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Maria Berry, resident of Wodonga on the border of </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Victoria and New South Wales, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">says that the demand for quality health services </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">already exceeds the amount of available clinics</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> around her. "In our area there is only one</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> Geriatrician, with a long waiting list of people</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> wanting assessments and specialist advice prior to</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> entering aged care."</span></p>

<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 24pt;"><strong>Additional costs</strong></span></h2>
<img class="aligncenter" src="http://35.201.11.70/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/old-450742_1280-1-300x165.jpg" alt="" width="440" height="242" />

<img class=" wp-image-79 aligncenter" src="https://findcaringarea.ga/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/1.6-300x152.jpg" alt="" width="460" height="233" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">People living in rural areas have to </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">invest more of </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">their time and money in order to be </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">able to </span><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">make an appointment at a </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">quality health care provider. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Ms. Berry pointed out that public transport </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">options in these areas are lacking, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">and with taxis being too expensive, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">it can be more difficult to find a carer </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">willing to make the day trip. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Some local services are not feasible in rural </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">areas due to the lower population, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">with options closing down over the years </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">due to staffing or funding issues.</span></p>

<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 24pt;"><strong>Uncertainty</strong></span></h2>

<img class=" wp-image-78 aligncenter" src="https://findcaringarea.ga/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/1.5-300x146.jpg" alt="" width="448" height="218" />
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Dr. Judith Anderson, senior lecturer at the Charles</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> Sturt University School of Nursing, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Midwifery and Tertiary Health, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">also pointed out the uncertainty of rural services.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> “The former government told the provider that</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;"> sixteen beds were sustainable and now they </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">are told it is not sustainable,” </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">she said. “Every time the government changes, </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">it will affect the community.” </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">Mr. Harding pointed out that the quantity of</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">services in many communities is not sufficient. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">“Many people are forced to move to </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">larger regional centers or near urban </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">areas because they can no longer get the support </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">they need in their hometown.”</span></p>
